Writer's block II

My hand's poised over the paper,
I don't know what to write.
Cuts my mind like my rapier
And gives my muse a fight.

The pen is mightier than the sword,
I suppose that it's just my luck,
As my pen writes on its own accord:
"This guy's brains has turned into muck."
